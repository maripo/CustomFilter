Keywords

The rule works when "elements to hide" contain any keyword.

You can chose two type of keyword. If "RegEx" checkbox is not checked, the rule works when texts in "elements to hide" contain the word simply. If you check "RegEx" checkbox it works when the regular expression matches the content.

Simple words are shown in blue strip and RegEx words are shown in red strip.

You can delete a word by clicking [x] button. When "Block Anyway" is checked, elements selected by XPath of "Elements to Hide" will be hidden, whether keywords are contained or not.
